## Detailed Notes on Key Findings

### The "FitCheck" Space Is Crowded
The research reveals a highly saturated market for "fitcheck" branded domains. At least 5 active competitors are using variations of this name:
- fitcheck.ai, fitcheckai.app, fitcheckapp.com/fitcheckglobal.com, tryfitcheck.com, getfitcheck.ai, fitcheck.to, fit-check-ai.com, fitcheckai.fit
- Many of these are full products with iOS/Android apps, thousands of users, and established brands

### "DripScore" Is Also Taken
dripscore.app is a direct competitor -- an AI fashion rating app with premium subscriptions and claimed 50K+ users. dripscore.com is a crypto platform (different vertical but still taken).

### Four Domains Are Genuinely Available
Only 4 of the 20 researched domains are unregistered and available at standard pricing:
1. **thedripscore.com** (~$10-15/yr)
2. **fitcheckai.co** (~$25-35/yr)
3. **getdripscore.com** (~$10-15/yr)
4. **whatsmydrip.com** (~$10-15/yr)

### Domains That Could Be Acquired

- **fitscoreai.com** -- Parked on dns-parking.com, expires March 25, 2026 (20 days from now). Could drop and become available, or try to acquire from Network Solutions registrant.

## Top 3 Picks

### 1. fitcheckai.co -- BEST VALUE AVAILABLE NOW
- **Status:** Unregistered, available immediately
- **Cost:** ~$25-35/yr
- **Why:** Exact brand match for "FitCheckAI." The .co TLD is widely recognized and commonly used by startups (stripe.co, angel.co, etc.). Clean, memorable, and professional. Register immediately before someone else does.

### 2. thedripscore.com -- BEST BRAND DIFFERENTIATION
- **Status:** Unregistered, available immediately
- **Cost:** ~$10-15/yr
- **Why:** If the app leans into the "drip score" concept for Gen Z appeal, this is the strongest available .com option. The "the" prefix is clean (like theverge.com, thecalltaker.com). Differentiates from the crowded "fitcheck" space. Pairs well with a tagline like "What's your drip score?"
